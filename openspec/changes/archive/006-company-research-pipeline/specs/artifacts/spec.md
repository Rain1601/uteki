# Artifacts Spec Delta

## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Company Research Artifact Set

Company research runs SHOULD produce `company-profile.json`, `financials.json`, `news-brief.json`, per-gate markdown artifacts, `final-report.md`, and `decision.json`.

### Requirement: Investment Memo Primary Artifact

For company research runs, `final-report.md` MUST be the primary artifact and SHOULD be displayed as an investment memo.
